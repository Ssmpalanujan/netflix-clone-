How can we help?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my password?

You can change your password from the Account page. Under the 'Profile & Parental Controls' section, look for the 'Change Password' option.

How do I cancel my subscription?

We'd hate to see you go! But if you must, go to your Account page and select the 'Cancel Membership' button under 'Plan Details'.

Why isn't my video playing?

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. For detailed troubleshooting, contact support.
